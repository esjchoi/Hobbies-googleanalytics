Basic Macaron Recipe

Ingredients for 30 macarons MACARONS 1 ¾ cups powdered sugar 1 cup almond flour, finely ground 1 teaspoon salt, divided 3 egg whites, at room temperature ¼ cup granulated sugar ½ teaspoon vanilla extract 2 drops pink gel food coloring VANILLA BUTTERCREAM 1 cup unsalted butter, 2 sticks, at room temperature 3 cups powdered sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 tablespoons heavy cream
